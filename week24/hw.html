PROJECT 3 Virtual Dream Space - DOCUMENTATION


01 Ideation and Possibilities

What kind environment it will be?

This dream space would serve as a personalized and meaningful visual representation of my core values, aspirations, and fears, inspiring me to continue pursuing my life goals with purpose and motivation.
To create a dynamic and visually stimulating environment, I plan to incorporate various geometric shapes such as triangles, circles, or squares in different sizes and colors. Each shape would symbolize a specific aspect of my life, such as growth or balance.

Which objects it will contain?

Growth and Progress
Ladders — represent the idea of climbing upward and reaching new heights, both in a physical and metaphorical sense. They symbolize my desire to overcome challenges and obstacles to achieve goals, as well as the importance of perseverance and hard work.
Plants —symbolize the importance of nature in my lives, and the idea that growth and progress are a natural part of life. It can also represent the need for self-care, and the idea that taking care of ourselves is essential to personal growth and development.
Fears and Unknown
Paths to different places — Paths are leading to a brighter and more open space represent the possibility of exploring and discovering new opportunities and experiences.
Relationships
House — represent my family and reminds me of the warmth and love.
Interests
Colorful shapes and undefined patterns—shows my passion for painting and diverse and dynamic nature of the creative process.

What would be the layout?

It would be a journey through the space. At first, it starts with the path, leading to the more open and dynamic main space. As for the main space, it would be divided into different section, with each section dedicated to representing different aspects of life and interests. For one section, it would be focused on cherished thing - my family, another one would represent my interest - creating. Also, there could be interactive elements that connect different sections, such as a ladder leading to a higher level of the space, representing the continuous pursuit of personal growth and development.

Consider the interactive possibilities in the space?

Ladders that allow the user to climb and reach new heights, representing the pursuit of goals and personal growth, and Interactive buttons added can change the colors or shapes of the geometric objects.

how would you like to structure the experience?

The tour could begin with an introduction to the overall theme and purpose of the space, followed by a path leading to different places.Once the visitors enter the main space,Once the visitors enter the main space, the guide could explain the significance of each object or element in the sections, and encourage visitors to interact with any interactive elements.
