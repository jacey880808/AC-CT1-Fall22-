PROJECT 3 Virtual Dream Space - DOCUMENTATION


01 Ideation and Possibilities

What kind environment it will be?

This dream space would serve as a personalized and meaningful visual representation of my core values, aspirations, and fears, inspiring me to continue pursuing my life goals with purpose and motivation.
To create a dynamic and visually stimulating environment, I plan to incorporate various geometric shapes such as triangles, circles, or squares in different sizes and colors. Each shape would symbolize a specific aspect of my life, such as growth or balance, while abstract objects would be created to represent significant life experiences and cherished memories. Additionally, I intend to include objects that represent my fears or challenges as a reminder of the obstacles I have overcome or still need to face, contributing to my personal growth and development.

Which objects it will contain?

Growth and Progress
Ladders — represent the idea of climbing upward and reaching new heights, both in a physical and metaphorical sense. They symbolize my desire to overcome challenges and obstacles to achieve goals, as well as the importance of perseverance and hard work.
Plants — symbolize the importance of nature in my lives, and the idea that growth and progress are a natural part of life. It can also represent the need for self-care, and the idea that taking care of ourselves is essential to personal growth and development.
Fears and Unknown
A black hole — is a representation of the vast and unknown universe, and can symbolize the infinite possibilities and potential that await us. It can be a powerful reminder of the endless potential and opportunities that are available to me.
A narrow hallway with doors — represents a more personal and internal fear of the unknown. The narrow hallway represents the feeling of being confined or trapped; as for the doors leading to a brighter and more open space represent the possibility of overcoming our fears and discovering new opportunities and experiences.
Relationships
Home / decorative items / photo frames — represent my family and reminds me of the warmth and love.
Two interconnected shapes, such as rings — symbolize the unique bond between me and my partner.
Some interconnected objects — represent shared experiences or memories that I have with my friends.
Interests
A globe —symbolizes my love of travel and exploration.
Turntable— represents my love for food and joy to explore new flavors and cuisines.
Colorful shapes and undefined patterns—shows my passion for painting and diverse and dynamic nature of the creative process.

What would be the layout?

It would be a journey through the space. At first, it starts with the narrow hallway with doors, on behalf of the fear of the unknown, leading to the more open and dynamic main space. As for the main space, it would be divided into different section, with each section dedicated to representing different aspects of life and interests. For one section, it would be focused on cherished things, another one would represent my interest. Also, there could be interactive elements that connect different sections, such as a ladder leading to a higher level of the space, representing the continuous pursuit of personal growth and development.

Consider the interactive possibilities in the space?

Ladders that allow the user to climb and reach new heights, representing the pursuit of goals and personal growth, and Interactive buttons added can change the colors or shapes of the geometric objects.
Touched Digital screens that display inspiring messages related to personal growth and motivation, allow the user to touch and switch.
Hidden doors or compartments that reveal new objects or spaces, encouraging exploration and discovery.

how would you like to structure the experience?
